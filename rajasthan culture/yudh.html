Tarain Ka Yudh (Battle of Tarain)

Year: 1191 & 1192

Tarain ka yudh Prithviraj Chauhan aur Mohammad Ghori ke beech lada gaya tha. Pehle yudh (1191) me Veer Prithviraj Chauhan ne Ghori ko buri tarah haraya. Lekin agle hi saal 1192 me hue dusre yudh me chal kapat se Ghori ki jeet hui, jisne Bharat ka itihaas badal diya. 🔍 (Click to Read)

Chittorgarh Ka Yudh (Battle of Chittor)

Year: 1303

Yeh yudh Alauddin Khilji aur Mewar ke Rawal Ratan Singh ke beech Chittorgarh qile par adhikar ke liye lada gaya tha. Rajputo ne dushman ka datkar samna kiya. Rawal Ratan Singh ke veer-gati pane ke baad Rani Padmini ne 16,000 rajputaniyo ke sath shahi Jauhar kiya tha. 🔍 (Click to Read)

Khanwa Ka Yudh (Battle of Khanwa)

Year: 1527

Khanwa ka yudh Bharat ke itihaas ke sabse bade yudho me se ek hai, jo Mughal badshah Babur aur Mewar ke Maharana Sanga ke beech lada gaya. Maharana Sanga ke sharir par 80 ghav hone ke baad bhi unhone datkar yudh kiya. Is yudh ne Rajputana ki ekta ko darshaya. 🔍 (Click to Read)

Haldighati Ka Yudh (Battle of Haldighati)

Year: 1576

Haldighati ka yudh adbhut sahas aur desh-prem ka prateek hai. Yeh yudh Mewar ke Sher Maharana Pratap aur Akbar ki Mughal sena ke beech lada gaya. Maharana Pratap aur unke wafadar ghode 'Chetak' ki veerta aaj bhi har Hindustani ke dil me amar hai. 🔍 (Click to Read)
