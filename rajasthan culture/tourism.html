Top Tourist Destinations

Jaipur

The Pink City known for Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort and royal palaces.

Udaipur

The City of Lakes famous for Lake Pichola and City Palace.

Jaisalmer

The Golden City known for desert safari and Jaisalmer Fort.

Jodhpur

The Blue City famous for Mehrangarh Fort and vibrant markets.

Mount Abu

Mount Abu is the only hill station in Rajasthan, situated in the Aravalli Range.

Chittorgarh

Chittorgarh is famous for its massive and grand Chittorgarh Fort .

Ranthambore

Ranthambore is known for its world-famous Ranthambore National Park and the historic Ranthambore Fort.

Ajmer

Ajmer's Ana Sagar Lake is a historic artificial lake.

Sikar

Sikar is a historical city in the Shekhawati region of Rajasthan.
