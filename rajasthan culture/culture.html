Rajasthan Culture

The Land of Kings known for its royal heritage, colorful traditions, vibrant festivals and magnificent forts.

Folk Dance & Music

Ghoomar Dance

A graceful dance performed by women in colorful ghagras during festivals and celebrations.

Kalbeliya Dance

Traditional dance of the Kalbeliya tribe known for snake-like movements.

Traditional Dress

Men's Attire

Dhoti, Kurta and colorful Pagri (turban) represent royal pride.

Women's Attire

Ghagra, Choli and Odhni decorated with embroidery and mirror work.
